# Publications & Community

## Citing quantmsdiann

If you use quantmsdiann in your research, please cite both the pipeline and DIA-NN:

> Dai C, Pfeuffer J, Wang H, et al. **quantms: a cloud-based pipeline for quantitative proteomics enables the reanalysis of public proteomics data.** _Nature Methods._ 2024;21:1603-1607. [DOI: 10.1038/s41592-024-02343-1](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41592-024-02343-1)

> Demichev V, et al. **DIA-NN: neural networks and interference correction enable deep proteome coverage in high throughput.** _Nature Methods._ 2020;17:41-44. [DOI: 10.1038/s41592-019-0638-x](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41592-019-0638-x)
